Amsterdam



Amsterdam, Netherlands' vibrant capital, enchants with iconic canals, narrow houses, and a dynamic blend of history and modern flair. Home to cultural treasures like the Van Gogh Museum and Anne Frank House, the city's open-minded atmosphere and bicycle-friendly streets make it a captivating destination that seamlessly blends heritage with contemporary charm.
